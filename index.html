The honesty contract: every number traces to a fluid-dynamics equation, a measured dimension, or a public standard — and ships with its uncertainty. Where the physics can't see something (a 2-D model pricing a hatchback and an SUV nearly the same), the tools say so on screen instead of hiding it. Costs are market estimates; the legal reading is flagged unverified until checked against the primary statute; and the coastdown kit exists so you can catch us being wrong.
